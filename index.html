Research

Hydrogeology & Water Resources
Groundwater Modelling & Seawater Intrusion

Numerical groundwater flow and solute transport modelling using FEFLOW and MODFLOW to assess seawater intrusion dynamics, coastal aquifer vulnerability, and contaminant migration. Field campaigns across 200+ monitoring wells with integrated hydrogeochemical and isotopic analysis to calibrate and validate predictive models.

AI & Environmental Data Science
GeoAI, Machine Learning & Explainable Modelling

Development of AI-assisted vulnerability assessment frameworks combining GIS, remote sensing, and supervised ML for groundwater quality prediction, rainfall–recharge analysis, and decision-support systems. Emphasis on explainable and physics-informed approaches that bridge environmental processes with geospatial intelligence.

Deep Learning & Computer Vision
Agricultural & Environmental Monitoring

Application of CNN and transfer learning architectures (MobileNetV2, VGG16, DenseNet121, InceptionV3) to plant disease classification and crop health assessment — achieving ~98% validation accuracy — alongside IoT-integrated real-time water monitoring dashboards for urban resilience planning.

◎

Long-term vision — Develop explainable and physics-informed AI frameworks that unify environmental process knowledge, geospatial intelligence, and predictive modelling for sustainable water, climate, and ecosystem management at scale.
